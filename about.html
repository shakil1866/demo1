Who We Are

The Girls School is more than an educational institution‚Äîit is a community dedicated to nurturing the potential of young women and preparing them to be confident, compassionate leaders in an ever-changing world.

Founded in 1952 by Dr. Margaret Excellence, our school began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a learning environment where girls could explore their interests freely, challenge themselves intellectually, and develop the self-confidence to pursue their dreams without limitation.

Today, that vision has grown into a thriving community of approximately 700 students from diverse backgrounds, all united by a shared commitment to academic excellence, personal growth, and service to others. Our alumnae network of over 5,000 women spans the globe, making meaningful contributions in fields ranging from science and technology to arts, humanities, and public service.

The Girls' School Advantage

Why Choose a Girls' School?

Research consistently shows that girls' school graduates are more likely to succeed academically, professionally, and personally. Here's what sets The Girls School apart.

The Research Speaks

Decades of research from institutions like the National Coalition of Girls' Schools confirms what we've known all along: girls thrive in single-gender learning environments. Our students consistently demonstrate higher academic achievement, greater self-confidence, and stronger leadership skills than their peers in coeducational settings.

At The Girls School, every classroom discussion, every leadership opportunity, and every challenge is designed specifically with your daughter in mind. There are no distractions, no stereotypes limiting what she can achieve, and no social pressures holding her back from speaking up, taking risks, or pursuing her passions.

Our students consistently outperform their peers on standardized assessments and report higher levels of academic confidence. In our classrooms, girls are encouraged to speak up, ask questions, and engage in healthy debate without the social anxieties often present in coeducational settings. This freedom to participate fully creates a stronger foundation for lifelong learning.

In single-gender classrooms, girls are more likely to pursue STEM subjects and report greater confidence in mathematics and science. Our leadership programs ensure every student has opportunities to develop her voice, take on responsibilities, and build the skills needed to lead with confidence‚Äîwhether in student government, athletics, or the classroom.

Without the pressure of trying to impress boys or conform to gender stereotypes, girls at our school are free to explore interests that might otherwise be discouraged. Whether she's passionate about robotics, athletics, the arts, or humanitarian work, your daughter will find a supportive community that celebrates her interests and encourages her to pursue them fully.

The bonds formed at The Girls School last a lifetime. Our students develop deep friendships with peers who share their ambitions and values. This sisterhood provides a network of support that extends far beyond graduation‚Äîour alumnae consistently credit their close friendships from school as foundational to their personal and professional success.

Our Journey

A Legacy of Empowerment Since 1952

From our founding with 45 students to a thriving community of over 700, discover the milestones that have shaped The Girls School into what it is today.

1952

The Beginning of a Vision

Dr. Margaret Excellence, a pioneering educator with a vision for girls' education, founded The Girls School with just 45 students and a small dedicated faculty. Located in a converted Victorian home, the school was founded on the belief that girls deserved an education tailored to their unique needs and potential.

Our Commitment

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

We believe that a diverse community strengthens the educational experience for all students. Our commitment to equity ensures every girl has what she needs to thrive.

A Community Where Every Girl Belongs

At The Girls School, we celebrate the rich tapestry of backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences that make up our community. We believe that exposure to diverse viewpoints prepares our students to thrive in an interconnected world and develop the empathy and understanding needed to be effective leaders.

Our commitment to equity goes beyond words‚Äîit's embedded in our policies, our practices, and our everyday interactions. We work actively to identify and remove barriers that might prevent any student from reaching her full potential.

Belonging

Every girl knows she has a place and her voice matters. We create spaces where authenticity is celebrated and every student feels seen and valued.

Equity

We meet each student where she is and provide what she needs to succeed. Our approach recognizes that fairness sometimes means treating people differently.

Justice

We create systems that are fair and representative, teaching students to recognize injustice and empowering them to be agents of positive change.

Empathy

We cultivate understanding and value diverse perspectives, helping students develop the emotional intelligence needed to connect across differences.

Mentorship & Support

Every student at The Girls School has access to caring adult mentors who know her strengths, challenges, and aspirations. Our faculty take the time to build genuine relationships with students, providing guidance and encouragement throughout their educational journey.

Celebration of Achievement

We believe in recognizing and celebrating the achievements of our students at every level. From academic honors to artistic accomplishments, athletic achievements to acts of service, we create opportunities for every girl to shine and feel valued.

Growth Mindset

We embrace the power of "not yet." Students are encouraged to take risks, learn from failures, and understand that abilities can be developed through dedication and hard work. This mindset fosters resilience and a love of learning that extends far beyond the classroom.

Service & Community

We believe in the power of service to transform both the giver and receiver. Our students engage in meaningful service projects that connect them to their community and develop empathy, leadership, and a commitment to making the world a better place.
