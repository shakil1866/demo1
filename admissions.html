Our Difference

Why Choose The Girls' School

An education designed specifically for girls creates opportunities that last a lifetime

Unparalleled Confidence

Research shows girls in single-sex environments take more academic risks, participate more in class, and develop stronger self-esteem. Without gender stereotypes holding them back, our students embrace every challenge.

  • Risk-taking encouraged & celebrated
  • Leadership opportunities abound
  • STEM subjects embraced with enthusiasm

Academic Excellence

Our rigorous curriculum is paired with personalized attention that brings out each girl's best. Small class sizes ensure no student falls through the cracks and every potential is nurtured.

  • 12:1 student-teacher ratio
  • Personalized learning plans
  • Above-average standardized scores

Leadership Development

Every leadership position is available to our students. From student council to club presidents, from team captains to debate leaders, girls step into roles that shape their character and future.

  • 100% student council participation
  • No competition from boys' leadership
  • Mentorship from upperclassmen

Supportive Community

Our girls form lasting friendships and supportive networks that extend beyond graduation. The collaborative spirit at our school creates an environment where everyone lifts each other up.

  • Lifelong friendships formed
  • Cross-grade mentorship programs
  • Parent community involvement

Your Journey

Admissions Process

Our step-by-step journey is designed to get to know your family and help you experience our community firsthand

Step 1

Inquiry

Submit our online inquiry form to receive information about the school, upcoming events, and admissions updates.

  • Online form takes 5-10 minutes
  • Receive welcome packet within 48 hours
  • Added to our admissions newsletter
Step 2

Visit Campus

Schedule a personalized tour to experience our classrooms, facilities, and community atmosphere.

  • Tours available Tuesday-Friday
  • Meet with admissions team
  • Observe classes in session
Step 3

Student Assessment

Your daughter participates in an age-appropriate assessment and classroom visit.

  • Low-pressure, activity-based
  • Teachers observe readiness
  • Half-day experience included
Step 4

Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents.

  • Application fee: $75
  • Teacher recommendation forms
  • Previous school records
Step 5

Decision

Receive your admissions decision within two weeks of completing the process.

  • Written notification via email
  • Acceptance packet with next steps
  • Enrollment deadline: March 15

Ready to Begin?

The priority deadline is January 15th. We encourage early applications as spaces fill quickly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions at The Girls' School

We accept students from Kindergarten through 8th grade. Each grade level has limited spots available, and we maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention for every student.

Yes! 28% of our students receive some form of financial aid. We encourage all families to apply regardless of income level. Aid is awarded based on financial need and is completely confidential.
